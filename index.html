DEEPIKA PADUKONE

Deepika's Information

Deepika Padukone , born on 5th Jan, 1986. She is a well known Bollywood actress and model. Deepika was born in Copenhagen city of Denmark. Mr. Prakash Padukone is a former famous international badminton player and mother Mrs. Ujjala is a travel agent where as her younger sister Anisha Padukone is a golfer. She has completed her schooling from Sophia High School and Mount Carmel College. After completing them, she enrolled herself in B.A. in Sociology from Indira Gandhi National Open University but had to drop it later on.

One of the highest-paid actresses in India, her accolades include three Filmfare Awards. She features in listings of the nation's most popular personalities, and Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2018.

Deepika started her modeling career with Lakme Fashion Week 2005 by designer Suneet Varma and won the Model of the Year award in Kingfisher Fashion Awards. She was also the part of Kingfisher Calender in year 2006. Her first television commercial was for Liril soap.

Deepika started working in movies from Aishwarya movies. It was released in 2006. It was a Kannada movie and Deepika was playing the lead role in it. Her first bollywood break held with Farah Khan's Om Shnati Om opposite to Shahrukh Khan in year 2007. It was a blockbuster movie which played a vital role in the establishment of Deepikas bollywood acting career. Later on she as appeared in movies like Bachna Ae Haseeno, Chandni Chowk to China, Love Aaj Kal, Main Aurr Mrs Khanna, Karthik Calling Karthik, Housefull, Lafangey Parindey, Break Ke Baad, Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey, Dum Maaro Dum, Aarakshan, Desi Boyz, Cocktail, Race 2, Bombay Talkies, Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani, Chennai Express, Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela, Piku etc.

Alongside her acting career, Deepika participates in various stage shows, has written columns for an popular Indian newspaper, is a prominent celebrity endorser for brands and products, and is vocal about issues such as feminism and depression. Her off-screen life is the subject of fervent tabloid reporting in India.

Deepika is now in a relationship with actor Ranveer Singh . She was previously engaged with Indian cricketer Yuvraj Singh and bollywood heart-throb Ranbir Kapoor respectively.

Production

She formed her own company Ka Productions in 2018, under which she produced Chhapaak (2020), in which she also starred as an acid attack survivor.

The romantic comedy Cocktail (2012) marked a turning point in her career , and she gained further success with starring roles in the romantic comedies Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani and Chennai Express (both 2013), the heist comedy Happy New Year (2014), Sanjay Leela Bhansali's period dramas Bajirao Mastani (2015) and Padmaavat (2018), and the Hollywood action film XXX: Return of Xander Cage (2017). She also received critical acclaim for playing a character based on Juliet in Bhansali's tragic romance Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela (2013) and a headstrong architect in the comedy-drama Piku (2015), winning two Filmfare Awards for Best Actress.

Personal life

Padukone shares a close bond with her family, and visits them regularly in her hometown of Bangalore. [156] She lives in Prabhadevi, a neighbourhood in Mumbai, and has admitted to missing the presence of her parents there.[7][157][158] A practicing Hindu, Padukone considers religion to be an important aspect of her life and makes frequent visits to temples and other religious shrines.[159]

While filming Bachna Ae Haseeno in 2008, Padukone began a romantic relationship with co-star Ranbir Kapoor.[160] She spoke openly about the relationship and sported a tattoo of his initials on the nape of her neck.[161] She has said that the relationship had a profound effect on her, transforming her into a more confident and social person.[7] The Indian media speculated on an engagement, and reported that this had occurred in November 2008, although Padukone had stated that she had no plans to marry within the next five years.[162] The couple broke up a year later;[163] she professed in an interview to feeling betrayed for a long time.[7] In a 2010 interview, Padukone accused him of infidelity, and Kapoor later admitted to it.[164][165][166] They reconciled their friendship while working on Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani.[167]

Padukone subsequently became reticent to discuss her personal life, but in 2017, she fondly spoke of her relationship with her frequent co-star Ranveer Singh, whom she began dating in 2012.[128][168]In November 2018, the couple married in traditional Konkani and Sindhi ceremonies at Lake Como, Italy.[169]

Off-screen work

Padukone performing at the "SLAM! The Tour" concert in 2014.

In addition to acting, Padukone has written opinion columns and has been involved with women's health and fitness magazines. She has also supported charitable organisations, and has performed for stage shows. In 2009, she was hired by Hindustan Times to write weekly columns for their lifestyle section; through these columns she interacted with her fans and passed details of her personal and professional life.[170][171] That year, she participated in the World 10K Bangalore marathon, which raised ₹13.1 million (US$170,000) in support of 81 NGOs.[172][173] In 2010, Padukone adopted the Maharashtrian village of Ambegaon as part of NDTV's Greenathon Campaign, to provide the village with a regular supply of electricity.[174] She visited Indian jawans (troops) in Jammu, for an Independence Day special episode of NDTV's reality show Jai Jawaan.[175]
