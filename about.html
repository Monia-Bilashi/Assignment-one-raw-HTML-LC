ABOUT OF DEEPIKA PADUKONE

Deepika Padukone

Indian actress

Deepika Padukone is an Indian actress who works in Hindi films. One of the highest-paid actresses in India, her accolades include three Filmfare Awards.
She features in listings of the nation's most popular personalities, and Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2018.

Born: January 5, 1986, Copenhagen, Denmark

Height:1.74 m

Parents: Prakash Padukone, Ujjala Padukone

Spouse: Ranveer Singh (m. 2018)
